FAQs

Ordering, Payment & Returns

How can I place an order?

To place an order, browse any category (Women, Men, Jewellery, Bags, Cosmetics, Shoes), choose the product and size/variant if applicable, then click “Add to cart.” When you're ready, go to the cart and click “Checkout.” Follow the checkout steps to enter shipping details and payment information. You’ll receive an order confirmation email with your order number.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit & debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and popular local payment gateways where available. All payments are securely processed — we do not store full card details on our servers.

What is your returns & exchange policy?

We offer returns or exchanges within 14 days of delivery for most full-price items (conditions apply). Items must be unused, in original packaging and with all tags attached. Sale/clearance items may have different terms — please check the product page or the Returns page for details. Start a return via your order page or contact customer support with your order number.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Delivery times depend on your location and the shipping option you choose at checkout. Standard domestic shipping typically takes 3–7 business days; expedited options are faster. We aim to dispatch orders within 1–2 business days for in-stock items. Shipping estimates are shown at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, we'll send a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's tracking page. You can also view tracking from your account's order history. If tracking is missing, contact support with your order number and we’ll assist you.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship internationally to many countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination and will be calculated at checkout. Please be aware that customs duties, taxes or import fees may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

Products & Sizing

How do I choose the right size?

Each product has a size guide on its product page (click “Size guide” next to the size selector). We recommend measuring a similar item you already own and comparing it to the size chart. If you’re unsure, contact customer support with your measurements and the product SKU — we’ll help you choose the best fit.

Are the product images accurate?

We provide multiple high-quality photos for each product, including hover/alternate images and a gallery when available. We try to represent color and texture accurately, but monitor/lighting can affect appearance. If you have concerns about a product’s look, reach out to support for additional photos or details.

Accounts, Gift Cards & Wholesale

Do I need an account to order?

You can checkout as a guest, but creating an account saves your address and order history and speeds up future checkouts. You’ll also receive occasional order-related updates and promotional emails if you opt in.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes — digital gift cards are available and delivered by email. Gift cards can be redeemed at checkout and are valid for the amount specified. Check the Gift Cards page for current terms and expiry information.

Do you support wholesale or bulk orders?

We welcome wholesale inquiries for select categories. Please contact our wholesale team at wholesale@atomstore.com with details about the products and quantities you’re interested in, and we'll follow up with pricing and lead times.
